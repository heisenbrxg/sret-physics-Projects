About Us

This platform presents a curated collection of physics simulation projects developed by students of Sri Ramachandra Faculty of Engineering and Technology (SRET). The projects showcased here are the result of hands-on laboratory work and academic exploration, where core physics principles are transformed into interactive, web-based simulations.

At SRET, physics education is strongly supported by well-equipped and advanced physics laboratories, enabling students to experimentally and computationally explore concepts across quantum mechanics, optics, particle physics, and spectroscopy.
